Chronological Age

To find chronological age, take test date minus birth date. For a child born on September 9, 2002, and being tested on January 8, 2007, their chronological age is 4 years, 3 months. Do NOT round up days to months.

Example: (Year, Month, Date)

Tested:
2007 01 08

Born on:
2002 09 12

age:
04y 03m 26d

Administer training page A for those younger than 4, or with developmental/language delays.

Administer training page B for those older than 4. If examinee age 4(+) doesn't get at least 2 training items correct, attempt with training page A until examinee understands process.
